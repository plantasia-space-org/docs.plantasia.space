Collections bring together Audios, Worlds, and Orbiters into curated experiences for exploration and transformation. They sequence creative elements so xPlorers can move through a cohesive journey.

## What a collection is

A collection is a curated group of content released under a single title and artwork. Every collection has a category — **Audios**, **Worlds**, **Orbiters**, or **Mixed** — and can carry a subtype, such as **Album**, **EP**, **Single**, or **Compilation** for audio collections.

A collection starts as a **draft** you can refine with collaborators, and becomes public once you **release** it. Released collections appear on your public profile and in the home feed, and can be starred and commented on like any other content.

## What this section covers

- **Release**: create a collection, add items, and publish it
- **Edit**: curate, reorder, and update a released collection
- **Interact**: find collections and play them as stations
