## Where to start

Go to **Release → New Collection**.

![New Collection](https://plantasia-prod-public.fra1.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/en/media/collections/new.png)


![New Collection](https://plantasia-prod-public.fra1.digitaloceanspaces.com/docs/en/media/collections/new-2.png)

## 1) Describe the collection

Give the collection a **name** and a **description**, then choose its **category** — Audios, Worlds, Orbiters, or Mixed — and an optional **subtype** (for example Album, EP, Single, Compilation, or Various Artists for audio collections).

If you need inspiration, you can regenerate the collection name.

## 2) Add artwork

Click or drag to upload the collection artwork — PNG, JPEG, or WebP, with a square crop.

## 3) Add items

You can add content from inside the collection, or from anywhere on the platform: every audio, world, and orbiter page has an **Add to collection** action. Pick the target collection — or create a new one on the spot — and optionally attach a note for collaborators.

Adding a different content type to a typed collection converts it to **Mixed**; you will be asked to confirm before the category changes.

## 4) Save draft or release

Save the collection as a **draft** while you curate, or choose **Release collection** when it is ready for everyone.

After a collection is released, later edits are published with **Release changes** — until then, they stay in your draft.
