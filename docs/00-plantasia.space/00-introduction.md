plantasia.space is grounded in the idea that listening is not a passive act: it is a way of relating to others, to the environment, and to oneself. For this reason, the project aims to move away from a one-directional, consumption-driven listening experience, proposing instead an open, participatory, living practice where imagination and technology meet to form new sonic dimensions.

This project invites us to explore together, to create, and to regenerate possible worlds through sound, sustaining a community that values curiosity, play, and diverse ways of listening.
