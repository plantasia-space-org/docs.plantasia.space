**xPlorer**
A participant who releases, explores, and reshapes sound across plantasia.space.

**Regenerative music**
Sound shared so it can be listened to, recreated, and transformed into new expressions.

**Entangled worlds**
Artistic representations of physical worlds that host Audios and react to exploration.

**Orbiters**
Spaceships that move through worlds and transform sound in playful, sensory ways.

**Collections**
Curated experiences that combine Audios, Worlds, and Orbiters for exploration.
