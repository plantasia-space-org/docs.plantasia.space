## Your home feed

Your home feed is where you find new releases, worlds, orbiters, collections, and clips
across Plantasia Space. You can choose **how the feed is ordered** with the **feed-mode
switcher** — the icon button in the **top-right corner of the feed**. Tap it to pick a mode.

The mode is separate from the row of content tabs (Tracks, Worlds, Orbiters, Collections,
Clips). The tabs choose *what kind* of thing you see; the mode chooses *the order* it comes in.

### The four modes

| Mode | What you get |
|---|---|
| **Home** *(default)* | A lively mix — mostly what's popular right now, always some brand-new releases, and a few older gems brought back up. The blend is lightly reshuffled each session, so coming back doesn't show you the exact same order every time. |
| **Recently updated** | Most recently *published* first — and a release bumps to the top each time you put out a **new version** (new audio), so the feed can follow a song's evolution, not just its final form. (A small text edit doesn't bump it — only a new version does.) |
| **Popular** | What people are engaging with most right now (plays, hearts, stars, and comments), with newer activity weighted more heavily so the feed stays fresh. |
| **Rediscover** | Older, under-surfaced releases that still hold up — a way back into the back-catalogue. |

### Keeping your choice

- Your selected mode is remembered as you move around the site.
- It's also in the page address (for example `?mode=popular`), so a link you share opens in
  the same mode you chose.
- **Home** is the default, so a plain link to the feed opens in Home.

### Good to know

- All four modes are available whether or not you're signed in.
- Today the feed isn't personalised to you individually — Popular and Rediscover reflect what's
  happening across the whole community, not your private history. More personal modes are
  planned for a later update.
