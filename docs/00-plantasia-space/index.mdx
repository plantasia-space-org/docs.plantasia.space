## Notifications

Plantasia Space can notify you by email and in-app. You control both from **Notification Preferences**:

1. Open the account menu and choose **Notifications**, or go to **Settings → Notification Preferences**.
2. Pick the **Email** or **In-app** tab.
3. Turn each category on or off — **Newsletter**, **Product updates**, **News**, **Beta program**, and **Community activity** — or use the global switch to silence all non-essential notifications at once.
4. Select **Save preferences** — changes apply immediately after you save.

Essential account, security, and transaction emails are always sent. Every notification email also includes an unsubscribe link that takes you back to these preferences.
